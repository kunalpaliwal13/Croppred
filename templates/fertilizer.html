  • Urea

    Urea is a nitrogen-rich fertilizer with a composition of 46-0-0, containing 46% nitrogen and no phosphorus or potassium. Widely recognized for its high nitrogen content, urea is commonly used to promote vigorous vegetative growth, enhance leaf development, and boost overall plant nitrogen levels. It is a versatile fertilizer suitable for various crops during different growth stages.
